My Favorite Holiday Destination: Bali

Bali is an Indonesian island known for its beautiful beaches, lush greenery, and rich culture. It's a popular destination for tourists looking to relax and explore Southeast Asia.


Top 5 Attractions in Bali

  1. Ubud Monkey Forest - a nature reserve and Hindu temple complex where you can see hundreds of monkeys up close.
  2. Mount Batur - an active volcano that offers hiking trails and stunning views of the surrounding landscape.
  3. Tanah Lot Temple - a Hindu temple perched on a rocky outcrop in the sea, offering beautiful sunset views.
  4. Uluwatu Temple - a cliffside temple with stunning ocean views and traditional Balinese dance performances.
  5. Tegallalang Rice Terraces - a scenic area of rice paddies that offer a glimpse into traditional Balinese farming culture.

Must-Try Foods in Bali

  • Nasi Goreng - a traditional Indonesian fried rice dish with vegetables, meat or shrimp, and spices.
  • Sate - grilled meat skewers served with peanut sauce and rice cakes.
  • Babi Guling - a spit-roasted pig dish that's a specialty in Bali.
  • Nasi Campur - a mixed rice dish with various meats, vegetables, and spices.
  • Bakso - meatball soup that's a popular street food in Bali.
